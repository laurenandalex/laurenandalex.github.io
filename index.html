Getting There

By Plane
  • The closest airport is BWI Marshall Airport.
  • Southwest is a preferred airline from BWI, so be sure to check the Southwest schedules for flight availability.
  • Rental cars are readily available, as well as an optional shuttle service to Easton (see below).
By Train
  • Traveling by train from New York provides an easy way to get out of the city on a Friday afternoon, though you will still need to rent a car or consider the shuttle service highlighted below to travel from the train station to Easton.
  • There are two viable options for train travelers:
    • Option 1: Arrive in the BWI Marshall Airport Train Station, then rent a car or take the shuttle service to Easton (approximately 1+ hour drive)
    • Option 2: Arrive in the Wilmington, Delaware Train Station, then rent a car to drive to Easton (approximately 1.5+ hour drive)
By Car
  • The town of Easton is approximately 1 to 1.5 hour drive from the Baltimore/Washington area, and approximately 3.5 to 4 hours from NYC.
  • On Friday evening, we encourage you to allow extra time for rush hour traffic along I-95.
By Shuttle from BWI
  • For those traveling from out of town, we recommend renting a car, though it is not a necessity if you are staying at the Tidewater Inn. The hotel is walking distance to welcome drinks and local activities, and transportation will be provided from the Tidewater Inn and Holiday Inn Express for the wedding events on Saturday.
  • If you are staying at the Tidewater Inn and prefer to not rent a car, there is a shuttle service called the BayRunner Shuttle running frequent trips from BWI to Easton and back. Reservations are required ahead of time and can be made on the BayRunner website here or by emailing Reservations@bayrunnershuttle.com.
